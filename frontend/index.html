How It Works
01
You Sign
Connect MetaMask and call requestScore() on-chain. Your wallet initiates the transaction — not ours.
02
Oracle Listens
Kwala detects the ScoreRequested event and triggers our off-chain API to analyse your history across 3 chains.
03
Score Minted
Your score (300–850) is written back on-chain and minted as a non-transferable SBT directly to your wallet address.
04
Lenders Query
DeFi protocols and fintechs query your SBT score via API to make lending decisions — without a bank account.
