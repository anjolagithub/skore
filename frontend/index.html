How It Works
01
Request Score
Submit your wallet address. Our oracle reads your on-chain history across Ethereum, Polygon, and Base.
02
Algorithm Runs
We analyse wallet age, transaction consistency, DeFi participation, token diversity, and cross-chain activity.
03
Score Minted
Your score (300–850) is stored as a non-transferable Soulbound Token on Base Sepolia. Permanently yours.
04
Lenders Query
Fintechs and DeFi protocols query your score via API to make lending decisions — without a bank account.
